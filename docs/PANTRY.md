# SPRAXXX Pantry

## Purpose
The Pantry exists to **feed people**, support learning, and provide access without extraction.

This domain is charitable by design and permanently separated from business operations.

---

## What the Pantry Is
- Human-first
- Non-profit
- Non-extractive
- Transparent
- Ledgered
- Voluntary

Measured in **humans helped**, not revenue.

---

## What the Pantry Is NOT
- Not marketing
- Not collateral
- Not leverage
- Not tax theater
- Not a growth hack

No ROI is expected or required.

---

## Funding Rules
- No mandatory giving below scale
- Voluntary contribution only
- Maximum contribution cap: **25% after billion-scale success**
- Pantry assets are never reclaimed

---

## Protected Assets
Once an asset enters the Pantry:
- It cannot return to Business
- It cannot be monetized
- It cannot be licensed for profit
- It cannot be pledged or sold

---

## Examples
- Free tools
- Community infrastructure
- Education
- Food access
- Emergency support
- Open learning materials

---

## Doctrine
**The builder eats.
Then the builder feeds others.**

No flex.
No guilt.
Just service.
