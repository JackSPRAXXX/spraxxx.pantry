# SPRAXXX Governance Model

## Core Principle
> Give to Caesar what is Caesar's.
> Give to God what is God's.

Business and charity must never be mixed.

---

## Domains

### Business (Caesar)
- For-profit
- Contractual
- Licensed
- Taxed
- Audited

Purpose: sustainability, infrastructure, innovation.

---

### Pantry (God)
- Charitable
- Voluntary
- Non-extractive
- Ledgered
- Human-first

Purpose: service, access, dignity.

---

### Future / Reserved
- Unclassified
- Experimental
- Non-monetized
- Non-promised

Purpose: prevent premature exploitation or accidental charity.

---

## Transfer Rules
- Transfers require an explicit logged decision
- No automatic flow between domains
- Museum records decisions, not intentions

---

## Financial Covenant
- Builder retains ownership and profit
- Charity is intentional, capped, and sustainable
- No forced sacrifice economics

---

## Why This Works
- Legal clarity
- Moral clarity
- Long-term durability
- Trust preservation

---

## Governance Motto
**Strong boundaries create honest systems.**
